What is this Club?

We are a group at Georgia Tech dedicated to learning about, experiencing and having careers in Themed Parks, Attractions and Experiences. Our goals are to learn more about the Themed Entertainment industry, compete in various design competitions, visit amusement parks, and help students interested in a future career in the industry by networking with professionals at different entertainment companies. Students of all majors and years are welcome and encouraged to join! Come to our weekly meetings Mondays @ 6:30 in Skiles 254 to learn more and how you can be a part of our team.
