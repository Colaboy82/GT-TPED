Speakers & Trips

Throughout the semester, we invite different speakers or organizations to talk to GT TPED about their experiences in their themed industry and what advice they have to offer to students like us trying to get into the industry.

Speakers are from companies such as:

  • Disney Imagineering
  • Universal Creative

We also visit many themed entertainment attractions and conferences to expose ourselves to more parts of this industry and gain more knowledge.

Some visited locations include:

  • Escape Rooms
  • Ohio State's TPEG SITE

If you or your business would like to coordinate a visit with GT TPED or invite us to your attraction, do not hesitate to email us!
